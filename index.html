How Does CarboFix Work?

AMP-activated protein kinase or just AMPk is the “switch” that controls how good our metabolism works. Most of us have this “switch” turned off and the metabolism suffers as a result. The reason behind this is the fructans found in “healthy” fruits, veggies, and legumes. These fructans start a chain reaction that makes our body produce less AMPk and thus slows down metabolism. CarboFix is built around the idea of helping the body overcoming this and returning the metabolism back to normal.
